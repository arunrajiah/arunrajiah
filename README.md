## What I'm building

### [perch](https://github.com/arunrajiah/perch) — Bird-station companion app
Your backyard bird station, on your phone. A companion app for BirdNET-Pi, BirdNET-Go, and BirdWeather stations.

For citizen scientists, birders, and conservation programs running BirdNET-family stations.

### [speciesnet-studio](https://github.com/arunrajiah/speciesnet-studio) — Wildlife camera-trap review
Self-hosted review UI for SpeciesNet wildlife classifier predictions.

For conservation teams and researchers who need to triage thousands of camera-trap detections without sending data to the cloud.

### [cctvql](https://github.com/arunrajiah/cctvql) — Natural-language queries for surveillance footage
Ask your cameras questions instead of scrubbing hours of footage. Works with Frigate and ONVIF systems.

Useful for conservation groups reviewing camera-trap footage, schools and small towns monitoring public spaces, and homelabbers who want a smarter Frigate dashboard.

### [openmem](https://github.com/arunrajiah/openmem) — Local-first AI memory
A private, searchable memory layer for Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini. Your conversations stay on your machine.

For journalists, lawyers, clinicians, and anyone whose AI history shouldn't live in someone else's cloud.

### [ai-auto-news-poster](https://github.com/arunrajiah/ai-auto-news-poster) — AI-assisted publishing for small outlets
WordPress plugin that drafts blog posts from RSS feeds using OpenAI or Anthropic APIs.

For local news sites and niche publications that can't staff full-time editors.
